Outdoor Obsessions Pro Shop

Outdoor Obsessions Pro Shop is a full service hunting, target archery, and fishing pro-shop located in Sierra Vista, AZ.

Established in 2009 by owner and operator Ed Miller, we are catering to the needs of hunters, trappers, archers, fishermen and outdoor enthusiasts of all kinds.

We participate in both local and national 3D target archery competitions as well as other outdoor industry events, and currently sponsor six staff shooters. We’re also avid hunters and fishermen. With Outdoor Obsessions involvement in the many aspects of the sport of archery and fishing, you can rest assured that we have the knowledge and experience necessary to help you reach your shooting or fishing goals.

What sets Outdoor Obsessions apart from other businesses is our commitment to you, the customer. We’re there to answer all of your questions and assist you with any issues that you may encounter, before and long after the sale. Whether you’re setting up your first bow or fine tuning your sights for deer season, we’ve got the tools and expertise needed to get the job done right. We’re focused on long term customer satisfaction and strive to provide you with the best service possible.
